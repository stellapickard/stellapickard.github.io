hello and thank you for visiting!! [NOT MY TEXT] I’m a web designer and developer—what some might call a designerd. I spend most of my time coding user interfaces and handing off compliant and pixel-perfect templates for engineers to flesh out with backend functionality. Sometimes I get to create beautiful designs prior to implementing them and sometimes I do logo and branding work that require no coding at all. Whatever the case is, the goal is to create an awesome web experience.

Outside of work, I’m an introverted yet strongly opinionated nerd. Things I enjoy include interior design, Bea Arthur ice cream cone from Big Gay Ice Cream Shop in the East Village, my home-made Saturday morning waffles, and cuddling with my clingy cat Toby and my hyperactive dog Butters while binge watching TV shows with Ian. Things I dislikes include the entire winter and summer seasons, waking up early, and IPA's.

I studied music performance at Carnegie Mellon University, then took up wood working, began a masters in Design + Technology at Parsons, and now I design and code for a living and for fun. I keep busy and I get things done. Check out my portfolio , and my resume to learn more about what I do. - Cheers!!

What's better than mountainbiking?
Mid-week mountainbiking! I started mountainbiking in 2013 and I've been a trail junkie ever since. I even worked in a bike shop for 3 weeks to learn how to fix my own bike. Here's a video of me, riding Glentress Forest's trails in Peebles, Scotland recently. I've started my own female cycling clothing brand for women, dirt diva, which I sell online on a website I designed myself. I'm determined to get more women out on the trails! So far, I'm succeeding. Recently, I started writing a column for FullSuss - a monthly mountainbike paper read by 45000 riders and I also have my own blog in which I share my mountainbiking adventures. Happy ridin' chicas!
